Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Salesforce Component Highlighter is a Chrome extension designed to help Salesforce developers and administrators visually identify Lightning Web Components (LWC) and Aura components on Salesforce and Experience Cloud pages.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information, browsing history, or user data. All operations take place entirely within your local browser session.

Permissions and Usage

  • Used to inject highlighting scripts into the active Salesforce page.
  • Allows the extension to highlight components within the Salesforce DOM.
  • Saves user preferences (like color and toggle state) locally.
  • Enables right-click quick actions.
  • Restricted to Salesforce domains only.

Data Retention

No personal data or page content is transmitted outside the browser. The extension only reads Salesforce DOM elements temporarily to identify LWC and Aura components and does not persist or transmit this information anywhere.

Third Parties

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external services.
